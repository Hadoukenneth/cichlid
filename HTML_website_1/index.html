Cichlid Aquarium

Cichlids are the perfect aquarium fishes for fish owners who want a colorful assortment of dancing fishes. These social and aggressive fishes primarily come from two places around the world, Africa's Lake Malawi and the tropical delta of the Amazon River in South America. to their aggressive behaviors, an intermediate-level knowledge of raising fish is recommended for future owners.
